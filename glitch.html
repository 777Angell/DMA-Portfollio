Glitch

This series of images comes from the Joker in The Dark Knight. I think the glitches fit him perfectly because he represents everything a glitch is, unpredictable, chaotic, broken, and mysterious. Just like how a digital error throws everything off balance, the Joker thrives in chaos and keeps you uneasy, never knowing what he’ll do next.
